About Us

Why we started Blackwood.

For years, we worked for other landscaping companies, and we kept running into the same problem: crews rushing through jobs and cutting corners just to get to the next one. We knew there was a better way to do this work, so in 2024 we started our own company built around it. Since then we've completed more than 60 yards across Long Island, and the two owners are still on site for every single job, start to finish. We always walk your property and talk through the plan with you before any work begins.

We take on jobs of any size, from a single cleanup to keeping your whole property looking sharp all year. We use quality materials and take the time to do the work properly. You get a clear price before we start, with no hidden costs and no surprises at the end.
